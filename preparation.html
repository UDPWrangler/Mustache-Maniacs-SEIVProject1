What YOU Can do to Prepare in Becoming a Computer Science Student

  • Strive to be a Life Long Learner.
  • Try to find a field or area of Computer Science that you really enjoy.
  • Invest yourself in websites, journals, or articles that allow you to learn more about the field.
  • Keep up to date on new technology and technology trends.
  • Practice any Computer Science skills that you are interested, there are plenty of tutorials online.
  • Take time to look at schools that teach Computer Science courses, compare them and determine where you want to go.
